How Nut Cut Peanut Butter Compares to Regular Brands

Most commercial peanut butter brands in the market are loaded with hydrogenated palm oil, emulsifiers, and excessive refined sugars to enhance shelf life and reduce production costs. This transforms a healthy fitness food into a high-fat junk product. In contrast, Nut Cut is stone-ground utilizing only single-ingredient premium roasted peanuts. You get 28g of raw plant protein, clean unsalted healthy fats, and high dietary fiber without any toxic chemicals, hydrogenated trans fats, or artificial sweeteners. It is pure performance fuel.
