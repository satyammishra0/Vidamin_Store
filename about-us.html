Our Story

FOUNDED BY NAMIT GUPTA

Ultra Fitness Gym was built with a singular vision: to create an environment where fitness meets luxury, intensity meets community, and goals meet reality. Owner Namit Gupta established the gym at Rajdhani Marg, Shuklaganj to offer a completely transformative experience.

With three growing franchises across Haibatpur and Dhaudhi Rautapur, we are expanding our mission to provide state-of-the-art facilities, certified personal training, and unparalleled aesthetics for every workout.

"Push your limits forward, aesthetics will follow."
